Tweet Your Bracket!

TweetYourBracket.com is the fastest and easiest way to share your March Madness picks with friends, family, followers and the world, all through Twitter! Make your picks, tweet 'em, and start bragging about your bracket!

How?

  1. Fill out your bracket
  2. Click the Tweet button in the top right corner
  3. Post the Tweet we give you*
  4. That's it!

*Make sure to keep the #tybrkt hashtag and bracket URL in the tweet so we can find and save your bracket.
