Frequently Asked Questions

I tweeted my bracket but my user page isn't working. What happened?

Wait a few minutes, if your page still isn't up, drop us a line on Twitter.

You do I know if I'm entered?

Go to tweetyourbracket.com/user/YOURTWITTERUSERNAME. If you're entered, that page will show your latest bracket.

I clicked the link to someone's bracket and I could change their picks?

With TweetYourBracket.com, anyone can post their bracket from any page, just by tweeting a link to that page. So you're not changing anyone's picks, you're just editing a bracket that's ready to be tweeted and entered.

I sent multiple tweets, which one will get entered?

We use the latest tweet you send, up to the tipoff of the first Round of 64 game.

When do entries close?

Entries will close a few minutes before the tipoff of the first Round of 64 game. Or more precisely, 2013-03-21T16:15:00+00:00.

Play in games? Why is it called the Second Round now? Do those games count?

The Round of 64 will always be the First Round in our heart. We will update the bracket with the winners of the play-in/first round games as they happen on Tuesday and Wednesday. If you pick a bracket before any of those games are finished, you are essentially picking the seed you think will win that game.

I have more questions!

We'd love to answer it on Twitter. We assume you've heard of it. :)
